How to Apply as an Exhibitor

Information for Potential Exhibitors from Overseas
Please understand that all information, applications and inquiries related to exhibition at TOKYO AUTO SALON 2017 will be handled in Japanese.
The exhibitors’ guidelines, which contains the exhibition notebook and regulations sent out after acceptance as an exhibitor, will only be produced in Japanese. We will not provide translations into English or handle inquiries in any language other than Japanese.
Please make sure you understand the exhibitors’ guidelines in Japanese and comply with the regulations.
TOKYO AUTO SALON ASSOCIATION will not provide interpreting services or introduce interpreter agencies.
Exhibitors are asked to arrange staff capable of understanding Japanese and/or interpreters.
We request that applications are made only by applicants who can understand and comply with the above.

Application Methods

１）Applying online
Please apply via the official TOKYO AUTO SALON website.
* S spaces cannot be applied for via the website. Always apply for these spaces using the application form.
2）Applying using the application form
Fill in the necessary fields on the application form, sign, and send to the following application address by courier.
* Always keep a copy of your application.
3）Applications will be accepted on a first-come first-served basis.
  • ・Booth locations for spaces A-F will be decided by TASA in order of application.
  • ・Exhibit locations for space S will be decided through a lottery.
4）Acceptance becomes officials once exhibition acceptance forms are sent.

Application Period

Exhibition applications are accepted from August 6 (Wed), 2014.
Applications will be closed as soon as the full allocation of spaces has been applied for.

Cancellation Fees

Regardless of the reason, cancellations made after the following deadline shall incur the prescribed cancellation fee.

  • ●Cancellation after delivery of the acceptance form: 50% of total exhibition fees.
  • ●Cancellation on or after October 31 (Fri): 100% of total exhibition fees.

Special Notes

  • ●TASA reserves right to cancel accepted applications and to refuse participation even after acceptance of exhibition fees for any of the following reasons.
    • 1. If payment is not made in full by the designated deadline;
    • 2. If TASA deems any exhibits or exhibitors to be in conflict with the show's objectives;
    • 3. If for any other reason TASA deems an exhibit to be inappropriate.
  • ●Also, please note that action such as booth cancellation after the designated deadline and late payments may cause an exhibitor to be turned down at future exhibitions.
