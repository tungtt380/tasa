Special Note
Refusal of Applications
All applications will be subject to approval by TASA. TASA reserves right to turn down applications at any time for the following reasons:
1. If payment is not made in full by the allotted deadline.
2. If any objects of display or the exhibitor itself does not meet the show's objectives.
3. For any other reason as deemed necessary by TASA. Also, please note that actions such as booth cancellation and tardy payments may affect the committee's decision in accepting future applications from said exhibitor.
